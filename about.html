About Me

Get to know who I am.

I'm Dr. Blondy Kayembe - Your Trusted Health Evidence Translator!

Dr. Blondy Kayembe is a health scientist and evidence communicator whose advocacy sits at the critical intersection of global health equity, outbreak preparedness, vaccine confidence, health misinformation combat, implementation science, and global health equity.

Why this engagement?

Because I believe that health equity begins information equity; that a parent in Beirut (Lebanon) or Kinshasa (D.R. Congo) deserves the same quality of health understanding than a parent in Geneva (Switzerland) or Washington, DC (United States); that good science, communicated well and implemented effectively, is the most powerful tool we have to improve global health.

Wondering why you should trust me?

Because I have the required education and skills to help you better understand complex health information, presenting as:

  • A Medical Doctor (MD)
  • A Research Scientist (Epidemiologist)
  • A University Teacher
  • A Healthcare strategist
  • A Public Health Consultant
  • A Global Health Equity Advocate
  • A Human Being and Taxpayer like you!
