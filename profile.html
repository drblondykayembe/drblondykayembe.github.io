My Professional Profile

Education, Experience, Publications & Engagement

👨🏾‍⚕️ Profile Summary

Medical doctor and epidemiologist with a PhD in Epidemiology from the University of Bordeaux and multidisciplinary training in public health, health economics, and health management. Experienced in integrating large-scale epidemiological and real-world datasets, applying advanced statistical and machine-learning methods in R, and translating findings into evidence for disease prevention and health policy. Research spans chronic diseases, emerging infectious diseases, and global health, resulting in over 10 first-author peer-reviewed publications and contributions to international collaborative projects. Recognized for scientific leadership, teaching, and multidisciplinary collaboration, with a strong commitment to population health research, communication and implementation.
